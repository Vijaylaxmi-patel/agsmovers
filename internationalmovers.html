INTERNATIONAL MOVES

As a leader in the mobility industry we operate everywhere in the world. With 49 years experience and an
extensive network of 146 locations across 97 countries, AGS International Movers offers personalised and
innovative moving services for individuals and businesses.

Making your move abroad a success is in our DNA  

Moving abroad with AGS International Removals makes the difference through its expertise and its top-notch client services. Our expertise ensures your move is a success no matter where you move to, no matter where you come from in the world. AGS assists you every single step of the way and guarantees a stress-free international move.

The safety of your goods is an absolute priority to AGS, which is why we have developed innovative packing materials and methods unrivalled in the industry. Our boxes do not only protect your items, they also respect Planet Earth.

At AGS, we are driven by excellence and continued customer satisfaction. Our teams are well-trained to take care of your belongings from start to finish, making sure the whole process is smooth and enjoyable.

How do we ship your belongings?

AGS guides you to choose the best solution to transport your goods. Means of transportation can be
combined to meet your requirements and your budget. For instance, essential goods can be shipped by air
while your bulky furniture will transit by sea.

AIR TRANSPORT

We guarantee the best transit times for your international move by having a dedicated team to manage air freight removals in each AGS branch.

SEA TRANSPORT

AGS exports more than 500,000 m3 every year worldwide and works with the most reliable freight companies to any destination abroad. We have also launched a group move platform.

ROAD TRANSPORT

We constantly invest in our fleet to offer you the most secure and flexible moving services. We pride ourselves in the AGS Liftvan, a specific container dedicated to urban removals.

What is so unique about AGS International Removals?

QUALITY/COMPETITIVITY

Since you deserve the best, AGS international moving company offers you world-class standards at the best price. With the biggest network in the industry, we can serve you anywhere in the world with the same care no matter where you move. We follow the standards established by the most prestigious certification bodies in the moving industry.

The volume of our international removals enables us to negotiate competitive terms with shipping companies, so you benefit from quicker transit times as well as the most competitive prices.

INNOVATIVE & PROFESSIONAL PACKING

Good and solid packing material ensures your goods arrive in pristine condition.

Over the years, AGS has developed its own packing materials to perfectly protect your personal effects. From dishes to LED TV or halogen lamps, we have tailor-made boxes to answer all your needs.

Do you have unique items or art pieces to move? Our teams love to be challenged. We can create custom boxes to ensure the safety of your precious objects.

With the best packing material and our well-trained teams, your goods are in good hands with AGS.

ONLINE TOOLS

We have developed a range of convenient tools accessible online. On your personal online portal, you will find all key information related to your move abroad. Tracking, progress, documentation. All our tools provide answers to your questions.

The online tracking tool allows you to locate your belongings 24 hours a day, 7 days a week in real time.
